About the Program

MORE THAN A GAME

Skyline High School opened its doors in fall 1997 as the third and newest high school in the Issaquah School District. Perched atop the Sammamish Plateau at roughly 550 feet above sea level, the Spartans have built a proud athletic culture since day one.

The Skyline basketball program competes in the 4A KingCo Conference — one of the most competitive leagues in the state of Washington. Playing against regional rivals like Issaquah Eagles, Bellevue Wolverines, Mount Si Wildcats, and Newport Knights, the Spartans face top-tier competition every night.

Beyond the scoreboard, Skyline is known for academic excellence, boasting a 95.1% on-time graduation rate and offering the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma program. Spartan athletes are students first — competing at the highest level on and off the court.
